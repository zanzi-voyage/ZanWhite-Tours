Zanzibar Tours Gallery

Stone Town Tour

Discover the heart of Zanzibar in Stone Town — a UNESCO World Heritage Site rich in history, culture, and Swahili architecture. Walk through narrow streets filled with historic doors, bustling markets, and local life. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Old Fort, Freddie Mercury House, and the House of Wonders, while learning the fascinating stories that shaped Zanzibar’s past.

Prison Island Tour

Visit the beautiful Prison Island, also known as Changuu Island, famous for its giant Aldabra tortoises and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Learn about the island’s fascinating history, relax on the white sandy beach, and enjoy swimming or snorkeling in the surrounding coral reefs. It’s a perfect short escape from Stone Town with unforgettable views and experiences.

Spice Farm Tour

Experience the famous Spice Farm Tour and discover why Zanzibar is known as the “Spice Island.” Walk through lush plantations and see, smell, and taste fresh spices such as cloves, vanilla, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Learn how spices are grown and used in local cuisine and medicine, while enjoying a rich cultural experience with friendly local guides.

Safari Blue Tour

Enjoy a full-day ocean adventure on the famous Safari Blue Tour. Sail on a traditional dhow boat across crystal-clear waters, explore hidden sandbanks, and snorkel in colorful coral reefs. Relax, swim, and enjoy a delicious seafood lunch on a tropical island, surrounded by stunning views of Zanzibar’s marine life and turquoise sea.

Nungwi Beach Tour

Relax and unwind on the stunning white sandy beaches of Nungwi, one of Zanzibar’s most beautiful coastal destinations. Enjoy crystal-clear turquoise waters, swimming, sunbathing, and vibrant beach life. Nungwi is also famous for its breathtaking sunsets, traditional dhow boats, and lively atmosphere — perfect for beach lovers and photographers.

Kendwa Beach Tour

Enjoy breathtaking sunsets and beach vibes.

Jozani Forest Tour

Explore the lush Jozani Forest, home to the rare red colobus monkeys found only in Zanzibar. Walk through natural forest trails and mangrove boardwalks while learning about local wildlife and conservation efforts. This tour offers a peaceful nature experience and a unique opportunity to see Zanzibar’s diverse flora and fauna up close.

Dolphin Tour

Enjoy an unforgettable Dolphin Tour along Zanzibar’s beautiful coastline, where you may see and swim near dolphins in their natural habitat. Cruise the clear blue waters, take amazing photos, and learn about marine life from experienced local guides. This tour offers an exciting and memorable ocean experience for nature lovers.

Sunset Dhow Cruise

Experience a magical Sunset Dhow Cruise along Zanzibar’s coastline aboard a traditional wooden dhow. Relax as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean, enjoy refreshing drinks, gentle sea breeze, and stunning golden views. This peaceful and romantic experience is perfect for couples, friends, and anyone looking to enjoy Zanzibar’s natural beauty.
